Carlos Sainz

Carlos Sainz, full name Carlos Sainz Vázquez de Castro, is a Spanish professional racing driver. He was born on September 1, 1994, in Madrid, Spain. Sainz comes from a motorsport family, as his father, Carlos Sainz Sr., is a two-time World Rally Champion.

Charles Leclerc

Charles Leclerc, a professional racing driver from Monaco. He was born on October 16, 1997. Leclerc gained recognition for his impressive performances in junior racing categories before making his way to Formula One. Leclerc made his Formula One debut in 2018, driving for the Sauber team (which later became Alfa Romeo Racing).

Races

Spanish Grand Prix

Barcelona

The Spanish Grand Prix, known as the Gran Premio de España in Spanish, is a Formula One race held annually in Spain. It is one of the oldest and most traditional races on the Formula One calendar. The race has been hosted at the Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya, located in Montmelo, Catalonia, since 1991.

Monaco Grand Prix

Monte Carlo

The Monaco Grand Prix, or Grand Prix de Monaco in French, is one of the most prestigious and iconic Formula One races in the world. It is held annually on the streets of Monte Carlo, the glamorous city-state of Monaco. The race is known for its rich history, challenging circuit layout, and glamorous atmosphere.

Miami Grand Prix

Miami

The Miami Grand Prix is a relatively new addition to the Formula One calendar. The race is set to be held in Miami, Florida, in the United States. It was announced in 2019 that Miami would host a Formula One race as part of a multi-year agreement.

Azerbaijan Grand Prix

Baku

The Azerbaijan Grand Prix, also known as the Grand Prix of Azerbaijan, is a Formula One race held in Baku, the capital city of Azerbaijan. The race made its debut on the Formula One calendar in 2016 and has quickly become known for its dramatic and unpredictable nature.

Australian Grand Prix

Melbourne

The Australian Grand Prix is a Formula One race held annually in Australia. It is part of the Formula One World Championship and typically serves as the season-opening race. The event takes place at the Melbourne Grand Prix Circuit, located in Albert Park, Melbourne.
